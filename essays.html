Essays

This page contains essays and papers I wrote for various graduate classes at UW, mostly my astrobiology classes. These essays draw from literature extant at the time they were written and summarize an astrobiological or astronomical subject of interest. These are not exercises in original research, though I have taken effort to synthesize and make critical comments. I offer these online in the hope they may help those interested in these topics on a casual or academic level who are just getting started. I make no guarantees in regard to their content or conclusions, though I'd be happy to discuss them with you (my email address is on the right).
